Where it works

Linkstream supports links from a wide range of sites. A few categories:

Video platforms most common

Long-form videos with multiple resolutions to choose from, up to 4K where available.

Short clips & social 1-2 qualities

Short videos from social apps — usually offered in one or two resolutions.

Audio & music audio only

Tracks and podcasts come through as audio files at the best available bitrate.

Direct video files simplest

Links that point straight at a video file download instantly.
